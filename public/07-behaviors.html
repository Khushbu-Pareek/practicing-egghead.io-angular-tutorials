Behaviors

When creating behaviors which will be used via attributes, it's best to keep their name generic, describing when the behavior happens rather than what the behavior does.

In the example here, the behavior names are enter and leave instead of grow and shrink. This makes room for a more re-usable code.

<div class="circle" enter="zoom-double" leave>
    <h2 class="message">Hover over and I'll Grow!</h2>
</div>
